I'm Rasheeq Ishmam, currently pursuing a Master's in Computer Science with a focus on Cybersecurity at Florida State University.

Previously, I completed my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering from North South University and worked as a Software Engineer at Nippzon Technologies, where I developed AI-based tools for Japan’s broadcasting industry, including video and audio anomaly detection and automated Japanese text correction. I have experience with C++, Python, Swift, and Solidity, as well as tools like SwiftUI, Docker, Git, and PostgreSQL.

Outside of academics, I’ve earned recognition at multiple national and international hackathons, including HackNSU and the KIBO Robot Programming Challenge by JAXA. I’m passionate about solving real-world problems using secure, scalable technology.

My research interests include Pattern Recognition, Graph Neural Networks, Bioinformatics, Machine Learning, and Blockchain.
